About Us

The National Society of Black Engineers is a formidable force in the pursuit of equity and opportunity in STEM fields. With a history that spans over four decades, NSBE has been instrumental in advancing the cause of diversity and excellence in science, technology, engineering, and math. At the heart of this mission is the belief that everyone deserves access to the resources and support necessary to realize their full potential. That is why NSBE has a Berea College chapter, which plays an integral role in fostering the growth and development of Black engineers and technologists. By providing a sense of community and belonging, the NSBE Berea College chapter enables its members to pursue their dreams and achieve success in their chosen fields. Together, NSBE and its Berea College chapter are making a profound impact on the world, and the future is indeed looking bright.


Our Mission

To foster programs that serve to stimulate and develop student interest in engineering; to strive to promote participation at all levels of responsibility in the field of engineering by the black communities, and to endeavor in the advancement of black professional engineers within the individual engineering disciplines at Berea College.
