FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the N.S.B.E's commitment?

To increase the number of culturally responsible black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally and positively impact the community.

Who can be a member of BC-NSBE?

Any Berea College student, regardless of race, ethnicity, nationality or religion can join.

What are the advantages of being a BC-NSBE member?

By joining BC-NSBE, you will have the opportunity to meet incredible, passionate and likeminded individuals from Berea College, from other scools in the region III that Berea College is a part of, and from across the U.S. You will also have the opportunity to attend local and national events such as the Yearly National Convention, various workshops, and the opportunity to occupy a leadership position at a local, regional or national level.

How to become a member?

Please get in touch with any of the executive members or any member, you will be assisted through the entire process.
